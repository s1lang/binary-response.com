24/7 Incident Response & Ransomware Recovery Services

Every minute of delay during a cyber attack increases the damage. Binary Response deploys senior DFIR (Digital Forensics & Incident Response) practitioners — not junior analysts — around the clock. We handle ransomware recovery, digital forensics, and data breach response for organisations across the UK and internationally.

Our Incident Response Services Include:

  • Ransomware Recovery - Containment, negotiation advisory, and clean recovery
  • Digital Forensics - Evidence preservation, attacker timeline analysis, root cause identification
  • Data Breach Response - Scope assessment, regulatory notification support, remediation
  • Business Email Compromise (BEC) - Investigation, account remediation, financial recovery guidance
  • Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) Response - Detection, eradication, dwell-time analysis
  • Cloud Incident Response - M365, Azure, AWS, Google Workspace security incidents
