Why We Don't Publish Names

Incident response is adversarial work. Threat actors are sophisticated, well-resourced, and motivated to identify the people investigating them. Publishing practitioner identities creates attack surfaces — phishing, social engineering, harassment, and retaliation are documented risks in this field.

We operate on a need-to-know basis. Our clients know who they're working with. Everyone else doesn't need to.
