Analytics Opt-Out

This website uses Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymised data about pages visited, session duration, and approximate location.

We do not use analytics to identify individual visitors. However, if you prefer that your visits are not tracked by Google Analytics, you can opt out using either of the methods below.

Option 1: Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On

Google provides a browser add-on that prevents Google Analytics from collecting data about your visits across all websites. You can install it from:

tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Option 2: Disable Tracking on This Site

Click the button below to set an opt-out cookie for this site. While this cookie is present, Google Analytics will not record your visits. Note: clearing your browser cookies will remove this opt-out preference.

Option 3: Browser Privacy Settings

Most modern browsers offer built-in privacy settings that limit third-party tracking. You can also use browser extensions such as uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, or similar tools.

Other Privacy Rights

For rights under UK GDPR (access, erasure, portability, objection), please see our Privacy Policy or contact us at enquiries@binary-response.com.
