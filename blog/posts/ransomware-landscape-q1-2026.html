Q1 2026 Ransomware Landscape

The Numbers

Our monitoring tracked over 2,100 new victim disclosures across 48 active leak sites in Q1 2026 — a 15% increase on Q4 2025. Active ransomware groups rose to 131, with 19 new groups emerging.

Key Trends

  • Larger groups splintering into smaller, agile cells
  • AI-assisted social engineering and phishing lure generation
  • Triple extortion growing — threatening regulators, customers, partners
  • Healthcare and education disproportionately targeted
  • Exploited internet-facing devices overtaking phishing as primary entry
