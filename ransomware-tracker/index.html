About This Tracker

Binary Response monitors 90+ ransomware group leak sites on Tor and the clearnet. When a threat actor publishes a new victim claim, our automated pipeline captures the disclosure and adds it to this tracker. Data covers claims from 2021 to the present day. Victim names appear as published by threat actors. Binary Response does not verify all claims. Some records may reflect exaggerated or unconfirmed disclosures. Treat this data as threat intelligence, not confirmed incident reports. For incident response support, contact our team.
