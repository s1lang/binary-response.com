Why We Exist

Every day, ransomware groups publish newly compromised organisations on dark web leak sites. Victims often don't find out for days or weeks. That gap is where the real damage happens.

Binary Response closes that gap. We monitor leak sites continuously and proactively contact affected organisations with immediate incident response, forensics, and negotiation support.
